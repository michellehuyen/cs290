Q: What challenges or troubles did you have completing this assignment. How did you work through them?

Q: What resources were most helpful in completing this assignment? How did you use these resources?

I used Codeacademy to understand the basics of HTML because I knew absolutely nothing about the language. I also re-watched the lecture videos and got help from my friend to understand how to get all of my code to show up on my website.
